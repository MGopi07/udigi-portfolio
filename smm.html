Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing (SMM) is a form of digital marketing that utilizes social media platforms to promote products, services, or brands. It involves creating and sharing content on social media networks to engage with the target audience, build brand awareness, and drive traffic to websites. SMM encompasses various strategies, including paid advertising, organic content creation, influencer partnerships, and community management. The goal is to connect with users where they spend their time online and leverage the power of social networks for business growth.
