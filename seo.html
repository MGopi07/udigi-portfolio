Seo

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility on search engines like Google. It involves various techniques and strategies to increase organic traffic, enhance user experience, and boost rankings on search engine results pages (SERPs). SEO encompasses keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, and technical SEO to ensure that a website is easily discoverable by users searching for relevant information or products.
